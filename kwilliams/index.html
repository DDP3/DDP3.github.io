Set it Off

Set It Off is a band that I have listened to since probably the beginning of my senior year. Originally, I had heard of them from a story I had been reading called "Kill The Lights", which happened to be the title of one of their songs. I looked them up and really liked the music that they produced. Unfortunately, by the time I became a fan of these guys, they were just about to hold a show in Portland... So I was unable to get tickets at such a short notice.

On March 11th, 2015 they had a show held in Portland at some theater place and I was able to go to that one. It was pretty amazing. I learned a lot of things from that experience and now I know that at a show if someone says to "make a circle", you need to run to the side, or you will be pushed. I totally got to touch Cody Carson's hand and it was the best day ever. The end.

As of recently, one of the band members has been kicked out of the band for reasons that I will not explain. It was pretty disappointing, but it was all for the best, and the band did a good job at keeping the loyalty and safety of their fans. By the responsible courses of action they have taken, I have decided to continue to support these guys, even through the tough times.
