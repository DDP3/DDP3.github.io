Welcome to my humble portal of geekdom.

This is Ariana.

I come in peace...

I'm proud to call myself a geek. I'm an avid gamer with a passion for pixels-- I love the thrill of creating, customizing, and controlling my own worlds, being the control freak that I am. Aside from gaming, I enjoy composing and playing music on the four different instruments I know how to play (Keyboard, recorder, ocarina, and violin), directing short films, and making artwork of all kinds.

The story behind this website...

This is a final project portfolio for my Digital Design and Coding class. I decided to include not only my Coding skills, but also other media projects I have done this year. I decided to make the website outer-space themed because it represents the infinite possibilities of my future and creative potential.
