About Me

Hi there, I'm Emma and welcome to my website! Originally, this site was only one page, but I added a game, form, and a journal so people could actually enjoy the website. It also had pretty basic design, with no bootstrap or colors. My favorite color is blue, so that's the reason behind the color choices (although a white and black layout might look a little cleaner and nice, oh well). I got a lot of help for Griffin in order to make the site work, so S/O to him. My personal favorite part of the website is the rock, paper, scissors game - specifically the pictures. They're what I call "invisible", meaning they aren't just a square picture, they actually blend into the page. I think it makes the site look more professional! I wish I could have made a nicer navigation bar with scroll-down menus and stuff, but that's something for the future. Thanks for visiting my site!
