About Me

Welcome to my website! My name is Maddy Shelton, I go to Franklin High School and play on the varsity volleyball team! This website was made for the awesome Digital Design and Computer Coding class I am taking. I designed this website with pictures, a specific layout, a game, and a web journal because I want there to be fun interactivity. The site is designed to be easy for you to navigate and look appealing to the eye. I learned a lot of coding so that I could put effort into this website. Knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap were necessary to make this website function. Throughout the creation of the Rock Paper Scissors (Big Bang Theory Edition) I learned Bootstrap and how to make a grid on the web page. Take a look at the form questions on this home page because they took a long time to make and it is always fun to fill out random forms! The pictures have borders to make them look nice. The four pictures are from many years ago and have friends, family and a beautiful picture of clouds I took at my first house. The game on the next page is interesting as well as re sizable in the window and the web journal was styled to look like it was always a part of this website although it was from another project.
