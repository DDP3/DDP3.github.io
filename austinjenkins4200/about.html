About Me!

So I'm a Patriots fan, and in all honesty I thought we were gonna lose, 20 seconds left, 2nd down, on the 1 yard line, the best running back in the league(Marshawn Lynch) in the backfield, I anticipated the 10 second time out the Seahawks took, only down by 4, but Pete Carrol made probably one of the most arguably worst calls in a super bowl. He tried to do a play they have done tons of time when they were in the red zone, a slant right through the middle, the ball got intercepted by a unrestricted free agent by the name of 'Malcolm Butler', this will most likely go down as one of the greatest super bowls in history. I know it had me on the edge of my seat the whole entire game, at one point when patriots were down by 10 I left the room, I couldn't handle the anxiety... But in the end we prevailed, even though there was a brawl at the end resulting in a 'Marcus Bennett' ejection.

I grew up in the neighborhood of Mt. Tabor later moving into my now permanent home in SE Portland. I attended 'Lents Elementery' off of 97th and Harold from kindergarten to 8th grade. I planned on attending Benson for highschool but later changed my mind after touring franklin, even though I was very hesitent. *Fun Fact* Freshman year I got into a conflict with one of my peers and got stabbed in my upper right arm, scary huh?
