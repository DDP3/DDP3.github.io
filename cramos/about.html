About hapteck.com

Hapteck.com was launched in February of 2015 as a blog site to share and talk about whatever the heck I wanted to share or talk about. I do all of the html and site work myself in order to bring about the style that I want for the site. And partly because I'm a control freak about little things.

On this site I usually write about movies I see, news I here, and things I'm working on. I will also post to the blog about whatever question people ask me through the site's email. I think community is important and am working vigurously on implementing more community friendly tools for the site.

I don't have any real hopes for hapteck other than interacting with as many people as possible on topics we both enjoy. That's what I truly love about online communities. Just being able to find a place to fit in so easily in an environment that's full of welcoming users.

If you have any questions, news topics, or other things you want to share to me feel free to email: hapteckentertainment@gmail.com
